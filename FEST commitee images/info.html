Teacher's Day




Forum Of Electronics And Telecommunication (FETS), Sardar Patel Institute Of Technology (SPIT), organized a grand celebration on September 5th, 2023, commemorating Teacher's Day for all the esteemed educators within the Electronics and Telecommunication (EXTC) department. The event served as a heartfelt tribute to the invaluable contributions made by these dedicated individuals, recognizing their profound impact on the lives of students and the academic community at large. During the ceremony, professors were graciously presented with mementos as tokens of appreciation, symbolizing the deep respect and gratitude felt by the students and staff towards their unwavering commitment to education and mentorship. These mementos not only honored the teachers' significant roles but also served as enduring reminders of their importance in shaping the future generation of professionals in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication. The event was meticulously organized and executed by the dedicated committee members of FETS, who spared no effort in ensuring that the celebration was a resounding success. Their passion and dedication towards acknowledging the exemplary efforts of the faculty members underscored the spirit of unity and camaraderie within the department, fostering a culture of appreciation and mutual respect.
