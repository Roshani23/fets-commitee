Alumina Connect
(online mode)




The Alumina Meet, organized by the FE.Ts. committee, served as a gathering point for esteemed alumni of the EXTC department at Sardar Patel Institute of Technology. This event provided a platform for alumni to reconnect, share experiences, and showcase their diverse career trajectories. Renowned speakers such as Gaurav Panchal, Maitre Vora, Siddharth Mohanty, Rani Tibdewal, and Arohi Shinde graced the occasion, offering invaluable insights into various aspects of career development. Their talks covered topics ranging from effective communication and interview preparation to problem-solving strategies and the art of selecting the right company. One of the highlights of the event was the spotlight on alumni's commendable initiatives, both technical and non-technical, that propelled them towards success in their respective fields. This emphasis on extra initiatives underscored the importance of proactive engagement and continuous learning in navigating today's dynamic professional landscape. Overall, the Alumina Meet provided an enriching experience for attendees, fostering networking opportunities, mentorship connections, and a deeper understanding of the diverse career paths available to graduates of the EXTC department.
