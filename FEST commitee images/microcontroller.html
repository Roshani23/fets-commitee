Teacher's Day




On October 27, 2023, Sardar Patel Institute of Technology hosted a dynamic workshop focused on microcontroller programming, tailored specifically for freshmen students. Led by the knowledgeable Adhik Mewada and supported by the esteemed F.E.T.S Committee, the event garnered significant enthusiasm from an eager cohort of 30 students. Throughout the workshop, participants delved into an array of electronics and computer-related concepts, gaining invaluable insights into the intricacies of microcontroller programming. Hands-on project experiences provided attendees with practical applications of theoretical knowledge, fostering a deeper understanding of the subject matter. A highlight of the event was the captivating narrative of Smowcode's inception, shared by Adhik Mewada. His inspiring journey resonated with the audience, showcasing the transformative power of passion and dedication in the realm of technology and innovation. Overall, the workshop served as a beacon of knowledge and inspiration, igniting a fervent curiosity among participants and equipping them with the skills and motivation to embark on their own journey of exploration and discovery in the world of microcontroller programming.
